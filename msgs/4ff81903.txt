With Discord not working wellfor us today, me and a few friends that hang out on discord most of the time, we have tried Jitsi.
I used it before for something I don't recall. Great piece of software. The noise supression is actually decent. One of my friends had some minor issues with it, but could resolve it on his own.
The settings are lackluster and we resorted to URL parameters as the UI does not expose many options.
One of my friends has a weird microphone that advertises stereo capabilities, but only play on the right channel. There is no way to stereo-to-mono in Jitsi or windows. All other major meeting/voice/video apps I know only support mono. Stereo could be cool, but most people probably haven't set it up correctly. Probably something I would contribute to Jitsi before rolling it out to friends permanently
