- The Web: The web platform that we all love is passionately protected by devoted adherents. The warrior protectors of the flame are themselves a double-edged sword. Change is intentionally hard. Opinions are strongly held. The dreamers become weary, worn down by high cost of change - both resource-wise and emotional toll. Any suggestion which rocks the boat immediately faces not just strong opposition, but sometimes immediate outrage. Campaigns of "don't hurt the web" mean well, while cementing the status quo. All of this ensuring that the three US-based billion dollar companies with web engines are not threatened. I ❤️ the web... becomes the hug of death. A browser can be just a browser. Cognitively and emotionally, a browser can no longer be other things. If you want other things, you're not talking about a browser anymore.
