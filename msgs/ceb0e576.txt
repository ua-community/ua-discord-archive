normally, it takes a set amount of denoising steps to reach a conclusion. but divisor does away with the idea of set steps and a known conclusion, and allows the artist to move forwards and backwards in time and extend image generation infinitely, so the conclusion is erased. on capable enough hardware, this means a near real-time experience of whats caled a 'latent walk', where an operator steps through different concepts, like a lens into the diffusion model
