Even with SWA as a goal, putting those capabilities in the web layer is where much the friction comes from. If Google treated IWA as a first-class app format instead of handwavily saying that it's "part of the web" or some future for the web, we'd be having a different conversation rn. You don't need the web for SWA. You need a portable runtime. Leaning on "everyone's got Chrome" is a trap.
