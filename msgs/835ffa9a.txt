I reverted these decisions, 

while it felt good to my controlling brain, it was creating unnecessary lag, esp with continuous indexing (some harnesses are jsonl, some are a db, some are combination of both).

The memory footprint of bringing determinism around time, especially with 2+ harnesses (with 3-5 concurrent session) was too heavy for my system, esp for an ambient agent with a 15 minute tick. 

So I let that go. And I am pushing more in the self learning direction, rather than prescriptive, if that makes sense.
