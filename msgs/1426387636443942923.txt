Conceptual synthesis

Agency is the ongoing ability to perceive, modify, and revoke the systems that act through you—individually or collectively—within conditions of legibility, negotiability, and reversibility.

Each concept—ad inversion, vendor representation, group browsers, situated openness, context engines—is a material exploration of how to construct and preserve those conditions.

The consistent political stance is that those closest to a system’s effects must hold the means to see it, shape it, and leave it.
