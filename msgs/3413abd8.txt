At the Vulkan layer image buffer = memory location. So yeah, if you work at that level, it's just a pointer swap.
Copying is really really cheap on the GPU (the GPU can usually even just do aliasing at a hardware level).
The cost is more in resources available. Memory space to a degree, and most importantly memory bandwidth.
