They’ve 3 orgs for aligning incentives:
1. DuckDB: core maintainers get funded by DuckDB labs 
2. DuckDB Labs: a consultancy / service arm that provides managed services to enterprise for deploying duck db and developing custom extensions. They get paid cash + equity both from MotherDuck
3. MotherDuck: they’re a VC backed company building a friendly and enterprise data analytics product based on DuckDB and is a customer of DuckDB Labs
