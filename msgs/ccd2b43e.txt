There is no way we can prevent people from using the Internet in general. ATproto serves a similar role as HTTP. You cannot prevent CSAM at the HTTP level, and it is a nonsensical idea.
What we can prevent is CSAM happening using our storage/PDS (and providers probably should do that) AND we can prevent CSAM infiltrating our feeds, profiles, etc and possibly by blocking it in the viewing client.
But we cannot prevent ATproto being used for bad things in general. it's just a protocol.
