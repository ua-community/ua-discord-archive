For the next alpha release, we’re switching our inference stack to llama-server from llama.cpp: https://github.com/ggml-org/llama.cpp/tree/master/tools/server.

We decided it’s better to rely on a dedicated inference server and focus our engineering effort on what differentiates Tiles: peer to peer collaboration and local-first features.

llama-server is still relatively new and was released as part of the recent https://llama.app work. If it had been available when we started, we would’ve chosen it from day one instead of building our own inference scaffolding.

It’s been working great in our internal testing.

We’ll be shipping a nightly build with this change in the next few days.
