Working within the platform you have has a number of advantages vs spawning new platforms. You can deploy and manage your workload at a single system level, within cgroups for example, to make smart scheduling and resourcing allocations. Yiu get great visibility into what is running.

On top of that, the world is your oyster. You have basically a programmable middleware between the program and OS, a proxy you are in full control of, that you can go to town building rewriting tools, observability, authorization checks to your hearts content with, in a way that's significantly easier than trying to do it all in eBPF or other system hooks.
